Traffic Flow Prediction

Introduction



Short-term traffic flow prediction is of importance for traffic control and guidance as it plays a crucial role in the time management of commuters and the route determination for the passengers.


There has been a steady increase in both urban traffic in recent years resulting in congestion. This project will help in predicting future traffic flow based on previously observed trends thus helping commuters to plan their route and also as an extension, can be used to control traffic lights.

We hope that this project comes in handy for the commuters.

About

This project, “Short term traffic flow prediction in a developing country using GRU”, aims at developing a simple way for predicting traffic flow considering short real-time footage and the past traffic trends using GRU RNN, which is made by four students from Govt. Model Engineering College, Thrikkakara as a part of their final year project.
